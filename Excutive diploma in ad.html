Executive Diploma in Architectural Design Training & Certification Course

Embark on a captivating journey into architectural design with our meticulously crafted Executive Diploma program. This 400-hour course seamlessly blends concepts, creativity, and technical proficiency, providing a multidisciplinary approach to building planning, design, and construction. Whether you're a seasoned professional or an aspiring architect, this program is designed to cater to your career aspirations.

Scope of Course

The Executive Diploma program trains students in Architectural Design that typically involves the use of specialized software tools such as AutoCAD, Revit, 3ds Max, and Vray. These tools allow engineers and architects to:

  • Create design and digital models of buildings and structures
  • Carry out design analysis and documentation
  • Optimize the layout of building systems

Career Prospects / Job Roles

Architectural design offers a challenging and rewarding career with excellent growth opportunities. The demand for Architectural designers who have completed an Executive Diploma program is expected to increase due to the growing need for sustainable and energy-efficient buildings, as well as the increasing complexity of building systems. Trained Architectural designers have a range of career opportunities in various industries, including construction, architecture, engineering consulting firms.

They can work on a variety of projects, including commercial buildings, residential developments, hospitals, schools, and industrial facilities. Architectural designers who are trained and certified in CAD tools such as Revit, AutoCAD, 3ds Max, and Vray and have gained expertise in the areas such as building planning, design, and construction, can easily get placed as:

  • Architect
  • Interior Designer
  • Design Educator/Instructor

Ideal For

These Architectural Design courses are most suitable for:

  • Civil Engineers who want to specialize in Architectural design
  • Architects who are passionate about efficient and sustainable designs of building systems
  • Construction professionals
  • Anybody who wants to build a career in Architectural design or construction management
