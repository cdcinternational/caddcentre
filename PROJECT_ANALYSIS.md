## 🎯 Project Overview

CADD Centre Kasaragod is a **premier design engineering institute** website showcasing comprehensive training programs in AutoCAD, BIM, Architecture, and Engineering. The website features a modern, professional design with cutting-edge UI/UX principles and is fully responsive across all devices.

### Key Highlights
- 🏆 **37+ years** of experience in technical education
- 👥 **2.5M+ professionals** trained worldwide
- 🌍 **350+ training centers** across 20 countries
- 💼 **100% placement assistance** for students
- 🤖 **AI-powered courses** in Smart Building and Product Design
