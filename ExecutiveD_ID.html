Executive Diploma in Interior Design Training & Certification Course

Master the art of crafting captivating spaces and set forth on a career journey within the dynamic field of interior design.

Program Overview

Explore the world of Interior Design through our comprehensive course. Starting from the fundamentals, participants will explore perspective views, design elements, and space planning, laying a strong foundation for creative design principles. The course progresses from design basics to advanced 3D modelling and visualisation, covering key areas like space planning, building materials, and crucial services. With hands-on training in AutoCAD, Sketchup, 3ds Max, Vray, and Photoshop, this course is designed to equip participants with a well-rounded skill set and hands on training for success in the creative world of interior design.

Key Areas of Training

  • Basics of Interior Design (Perspective views, Design elements, Space planning, Building materials)
  • Types of Materials and Uses (Types of masonry and uses, Types of finishes and uses)
  • Building Services (Plumbing, Electrical, Lighting, Vaastu)
  • Basics of Vaastu
  • Engineering Drawing using AutoCAD (Basic profiles, Layer management, Blocks and references, Layouts and printing, Collaboration and data exchange, 3D modeling, AutoLISP)
  • Modeling using Sketchup (Drawing tool, Annotative model, Groups & components, Sandbox and solid tools, Architecture & interior design, Plugins, Advance model in SketchUp, 3D model to 2D)
  • 3D Modeling using 3ds Max (Shapes and geometry, Modifiers, Modelling interior, exterior, and mechanical objects, Materials, Lights, camera, and rendering, Animation, Files management)
  • Visualisation using Vray (V-Ray image sampling, V-Ray lights, GI for exterior and interior scenes, V-Ray physical camera, V-Ray materials, V-Ray aerial perspective, V-Ray displacement mod)
  • Photoshop (Creating new canvas & customizing, Work space, Importing, exporting & saving files, Drawing, selection, editing tools, Layers & layer management, Working with images & color correction)

Career Opportunities

Upon completing the Executive Diploma in Interior Design, participants can explore employment opportunities in the following roles:

  • Architect
  • Interior Designer
  • Interior Decorator
  • Colour Consultant
  • Design Educator/Instructor

Program Highlights

  • Students participate in continuous project assessments and receive support at every stage. Completion of module projects increases their chances of attending more job interviews upon successfully finishing the course.
  • Mentorship: Industry-experienced mentors, certified by CADD Centre ITA, guide students in their career development. Monthly scheduled sessions facilitate this support.
  • Expert-led Sessions: Our Engineering, Technology, Career (ETC) sessions, led by subject-matter experts, offer learners valuable real-world insights and practical knowledge, keeping them up-to-date with the latest industry trends.
  • Career Guidance: A comprehensive career guidance program assists students in assessing their skills and readiness for the job market.
  • Placement Assistance: CADD Centre's 'First Job Pakka' initiative provides placement support to eligible students after completing their course successfully.
  • World-class courseware: Our meticulously designed course material upholds the highest standards, offering comprehensive subject coverage with practice exercises, integrating the latest industry advancements, and catering to diverse learning styles.
  • Learning Management System: Our robust LMS, accessible via a mobile app, provides convenient access to e-learning materials, certificates, and job opportunities.
