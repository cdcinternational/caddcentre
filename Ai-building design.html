Certificate in AI-Powered Smart Building Design Training & Certification Course

Artificial Intelligence is transforming architecture by optimising design workflows, reducing project costs by 20%, and increasing efficiency by 30%. This program trains learners to integrate AI in architectural design, enhancing visualisation, space planning, and photorealistic rendering. The focus of the training includes:

  • Fundamental Design Concepts

  • Perspective Views and Architectural Styles

  • Massing and Functions of Building Design

    Understand spatial relationships

  • AI-Powered Photorealistic Rendering using PROME.AI

    Generate stunning visualizations

  • Site Analysis and Space Planning using ARCHITECTURES.AI

    Optimize site utilization

  • Exporting AI-Generated Designs as BIM-Supported Models

    Industry-standard deliverables
