Master Certificate in BIM Training & Certification Course

Advance your career with the Master Diploma in BIM at CADD Centre. Our advanced BIM course offers extensive certification training in AutoCAD, Revit, MEP design, and more. Benefit from expert-led instruction, hands-on projects, and comprehensive career support. Enroll now!

Duration

  • Time: 3 hours per day
  • Mode: Part time/Full time/Intensive
  • Venue: in-centre Program

Program Overview

Master Certificate in BIM provides comprehensive training in essential areas of Building Information Modeling (BIM). It covers BIM concepts and standards, implementation strategies, 2D engineering drawing, and production drawing using software like Revit Architecture, Revit Structure, Civil 3D, and Revit MEP. Participants also learn about Common Data Environment (CDE) and collaborative project management with BIM 360. The program concludes with BIM coordination emphasizing clash detection and conflict resolution at LOD 350, preparing participants for real-world construction projects.

Key areas of training BIM

  • Introduction to BIM and Industry 4.0 Integration
  • Concepts using ISO 19650 Standards
  • LOD 100: Conceptual Modeling
  • Introduction to OmniClass and UniClass
  • Level of Information Need (LOIN)
  • LOD 200: Schematic Design
  • Engineering Drawing Fundamentals
  • 3D BIM Modeling
  • LOD 300: Detailed Modeling
  • Schematic Design and BIM Coordination
  • Level of Development (LOD)
  • LOD 350: BIM Coordination
